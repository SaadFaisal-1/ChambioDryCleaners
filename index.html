Who We Are

Your local drycleaner on Holloway Road

Chambio Drycleaner has been caring for garments across Highbury and Islington since 1977, built on straightforward pricing, honest turnaround times and real attention to the fabrics we're given. Drycleaning shirts, suits and everyday laundry is the heart of what we do, but we also handle the trickier jobs — leather, suede, curtains, carpets and sofas — with the same care.
